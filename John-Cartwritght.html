John Cartwright

In 1818, John Cartwright became the first person to settle in Madison. He went to the Tennessee Valley and liked what he saw of the land, and recieved a grant for land there. That land would later be named Madison Station, and then just Madison.

Fun Fact: John's younger brother, Edmund Cartwright, invented the power loom.
